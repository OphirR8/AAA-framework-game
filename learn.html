The AAA Framework of Reframing

  1. Acknowledge:
    • How: Repeat the prospect’s concern or question back to them.
    • Purpose: Build rapport and show active listening.
    • Example: If a prospect asks, “How many certifications do your trainers have?” you respond, “So you’re curious about the certifications our trainers hold.”
  2. Associate:
    • Purpose: Positively relate the prospect’s concern to successful behaviors or clients.
    • How: Link their question to traits of satisfied customers or successful outcomes.
    • Example: “That’s a great question. Many of our most successful clients had the same concern.”
  3. Ask:
    • Purpose: Regain control by asking a question that reframes the conversation.
    • How: Probe deeper to understand the underlying concern.
    • Example: “Which specific certifications are you looking for?”
